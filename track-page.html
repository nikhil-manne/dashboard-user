How Complaint Tracking Works

1

Submit Complaint

File your complaint through the portal or at a Booth. Receive an acknowledgement receipt with Application Number.

2

Commissionerate Review

Your complaint is forwarded to the designated Police Commissionerate for review and assignment to an officer.

3

Investigation

The assigned officer investigates the complaint. Status updates are reflected in the system as action is taken.

4

Resolution

Once resolved, the status is updated. You may be contacted on your registered mobile number for feedback.
